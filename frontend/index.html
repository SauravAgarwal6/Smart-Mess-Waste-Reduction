About the Project

Our Smart Mess Management System reduces food waste by allowing students to pre-vote meals and enabling mess managers to plan portions effectively. This initiative supports sustainability, cost savings, and student satisfaction on campus.

Key Features

Meal Voting

Students mark attendance for meals to help prepare accurate portions.

Smart Dashboard

Staff monitor preferences and food usage in real time.

Waste Tracking

Track leftovers to reduce waste and improve planning.

Notifications

Reminders for students and alerts for mess staff.

Sustainability Score

Hostels compete on waste reduction, encouraging eco-friendly practices.

Mobile Friendly

Students can easily vote for meals using their mobile devices.
